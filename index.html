    Our Love Story...

    If we had to to use one word to describe us... it would be passionate. For our entire lives we have been passionate about so many things; sports, school, backyard party games, etc. But we also feel that same passion for each other. From our first kiss under the stars on Thompson Hall lawn we both knew there was something there, and we have been following that passion for the past seven (almost eight!) years.

    If you gave us TWO words though, the other one would have to be weird. Now we are all a little weird in our own ways, and you meet and interact with many different types of weird people throughout your life. But only once in a while do you find someone who is a perfect match for your weirdness. We consider ourselves very lucky that we did! Because honestly who else would marry us if not one another?! We're so excited to be getting married and begin out life long journey with one another. We look forward to the future and all of the things it holds.

    If you know us at all, you know we love nothing more than having a good time with our friends which is why we're so looking forward to sharing this special moment with you! It would be hard to imagine where we would be without the love and support of our families and friends. So even though it's OUR special day, we hope that you have the most fun possible!
